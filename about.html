Homechoice International Group is a professional enterprise engaged in the home textile business. With an abundance of experience in the home textile industry, Homechoice devotes itself to creating better designs and harmonic experiences for all families.

Decorating your home with our unique ideas is the eternal goal we strive for. Homechoice is focused on manufacturing a variety of home textile products, including decorative pillows, cushions, curtains, window panels, comforters, table linens, and more. Based in the production centers located in China and India, and with sourcing and design centers located in United States, Homechoice International Group is ahead of the competition in terms of quality control and costs saving.
